Norwegian Railways

Over the years I've spent much time contributing to the museum railway in Bergen. The museum train is running between Garnes and Midtun every Sunday from Mid-June to Mid-September. If you need information about the schedule, charter trips, special events, or if you want to advertise in our train, contact me.
